About Us

This group has been set up for those people who would like to get out of the house, meet some great new people,
and have a crap-ton amount of fun playing some good old American touch football. This group is open to all ages,
physical conditions, and skill levels. This group is co-ed so that means ladies are both welcome and encouraged to come out.
This group is not for those who take the game too seriously as any who fight or show a pattern of hurting the game
will NOT be welcome in the future.
